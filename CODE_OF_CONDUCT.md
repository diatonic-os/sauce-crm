# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)**
as its Code of Conduct. The full text is available at that link and is
incorporated here by reference.

In short: we pledge to make participation in the Sauce CRM community a
harassment-free experience for everyone, and we expect all participants —
contributors, maintainers, and sponsors — to act respectfully and in good faith.

## Scope

This Code applies within all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions,
Discord, and any official channel) and when an individual is representing the
project in public spaces.

## Reporting

To report a concern, contact the maintainer privately:

- **Email:** drew@dacvisuals.com

Reports are handled confidentially. The maintainer will review and respond as
promptly as is reasonable, and may take any action deemed appropriate, up to and
including a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.
